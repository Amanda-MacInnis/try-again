Visit Upper Musquodoboit

Local Food & Agriculture

Visit one of many family farms and try out their local goods.

Musquodoboit River

Swim, kayak, canoe or fish.. or just enjoy the scenic views!

Castle Playground

Take the kids to play on Nova Scotia's largest wooden playground.
